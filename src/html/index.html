What Does Street Link Mean?

Every road and street in the UK has a unique CoronaFriend link, which is the sequence of letters and numbers after the # sign in the site's address in your browser's address box.

You can use this link to share with your neighbours and it will always take you directly to your street on the site.

What Does Help Needed Mean?

Help Needed means that none of this street has been covered under the CoronaFriend project. If you are able, please cover all or part of the street and let us know by submitting an update.

What Does Partly Covered Mean?

Select Partly Covered if you will drop leaflets only at some homes on your street. Please be sure to tell us which addresses you have covered below before submitting your update.

What Does Fully Covered Mean?

Select Fully Covered if you will drop leaflets at every home on your street.
