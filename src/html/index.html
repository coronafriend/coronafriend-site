Help and connect with your neighbours during COVID-19

COVID-19 is putting everyone at great risk; friends, families and the vulnerable. Everyone lives a few doors away from someone who might need help.

Offer your support by posting a Corona Friend postcard through their door. If we all pull together, no-one in the UK will have to face this virus alone.

Step One

Claim and mark your road on the map below so people know whether it's covered or not. Zoom in to level 15, enter your postcode and click or tap on Search or click or tap on the map push pin icon to use your browser's built in geolocation to find your neighbourhood.

If you're not able to cover all of your road, just mark it as partially covered. You can also add a note about what you've been up to which other users will see. If you know of any local websites, Facebook groups or other social media links, you can add those in as well.

Step Two
