Help and connect with your neighbours during COVID-19

COVID-19 is putting everyone at great risk; friends, families and the vulnerable. Everyone lives a few doors away from someone who might need help.

Offer your support by posting a Corona Friend postcard through their door. If we all pull together, no-one in the UK will have to face this virus alone.

Step Two

Download, print out and fill in the Corona Friend postcard.

Step Three

Pop the filled out postcard through your neighbour's door.
